Platform Deep Dive: Multi-User & Pricing

Multi-User Options

Uses a role-based system. The number of "seats" depends on the tier:

  • Free: 1 user
  • Essentials: Up to 3 users
  • Standard: Up to 5 users
  • Premium: Unlimited users

Pricing Tiers

Free~$0 / month
Essentials~$13 / month
Standard~$20 / month
Premium~$350 / month

Multi-User Options

Access is structured by plan:

  • Lite: 1 user
  • Essentials: Multiple users with defined permissions.
  • Premier: Unlimited users with advanced features like template locking.

Pricing Tiers

Lite~$9 / month
Essentials~$29 / month
Premier~$149 / month

Multi-User Options

Allows multiple users with different roles:

  • Lite: 1 user
  • Standard: Up to 3 users (Owner, Creator, Viewer).
  • Premium: Unlimited users.

Pricing Tiers

Lite~$12 / month
Standard~$35 / month
Premium~$80 / month

Multi-User Options

Has a distinct model for team use:

  • Educator Plans: Single-user licenses only.
  • Smore for Teams: Required for multi-user collaboration. A group purchase of licenses managed from a central dashboard.

Pricing Tiers (Annual)

Recommendation

Office of Technology Recommendation

We recommend upgrading Smore to a Smore Teams license in the short term so that you are up and running for the close deadline event.

Once that has passed and there is more time to consider a better solution, we recommend coming back to this report and investigating the tools listed to see what best fits your department's dynamic and budget.

Based on this report, I, Lucas Ballard, would personally recommend Constant Contact. It seems very capable without a sharp learning curve.

General Recommendations

For a team wanting a balance of power and future growth potential, start by evaluating Mailchimp.

For a team that prioritizes ease of use and a polished, professional look above all, look at Campaign Monitor.

For a team that needs to create visually impactful announcements with maximum speed and minimal training, Smore is the ideal choice.
